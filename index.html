ACES_Coders?

ACES Coders, organized by ACES, the Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya is the largest algorithmic programming competition in the country. It is a 12-hour coding competition open to all undergraduates island-wide.

FAQ

~What should I bring to the competition?

Participants are expected to bring their own laptops and chargers. WiFi access points will be provided. As it is going to be an overnight event, bring anything that is necessary.

~Can I attend ACES Coders virtually?

No, unfortunately, all selected participants will be required to be present on-site to finish the competition.

~What should we do if the head of the department did not receive the registration forms?

Email to aces@eng.pdn.ac.lk mentioning your university/institute and the department. They will get back to you.

~Will food and beverages will be provided?

Yes, they will be provided.

~If I have more questions, how can I clarify?

You can email your inquiries to aces@eng.pdn.ac.lk
